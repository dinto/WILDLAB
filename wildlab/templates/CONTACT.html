Join Us


Post-docs
We are always looking for talented post doctoral fellows with their own fellowships/funding. Visit the lab or write to us to explore opportunities.

PhD
If you are interested in a PhD position matching our research themes, write to us. Please note that having a fellowship is not the only criteria for securing a position.

Project positions
Vacancies in projects are advertised on the institutional website.

MSc dissertations and internships
Each year 3-5 masters students are accepted in the lab for their dissertation work. Please note that, we accept students with at least three months duration exclusively for their dissertation work. Interns and highly motivated volunteers from related disciplines are also welcome to join our research and outreach programmes. Contact us before making a formal application to the institute.

We accept interns throughout the year in our Biodiversity Internship Programme . For details contact us .
